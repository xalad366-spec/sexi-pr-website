bogus gateway · safe payment test

Test the entire checkout flow with no real money, no card, no bank info, no KYC. Shopify built this for exactly your scenario.

What it gives you: a real order in Shopify Orders dashboard, customer confirmation email, IVU calc, shipping calc, inventory drop — every part of the chain except the actual card processor. Auto-disables when Shopify Payments goes live after KYC.

step 1 · prep (~3 min)

Settings → Plan → Subscription confirmation → Add business address (placeholder name + Santurce address — gets overwritten by client later)
Online Store → Preferences → Password protection → toggle OFF → Save

step 2 · enable bogus gateway (~2 min)

Settings → Payments
Find "Supported payment methods" or "Add payment methods" or "Choose alternative provider"
Search field → type bogus
Click "(for testing) Bogus Gateway" → Activate → Save

step 3 · run test purchase (~3 min)

From sexi-pr.pages.dev, add a product to cart, click pagar
Shopify checkout opens → fill shipping with anything (any address)
At payment section, use the magic test card numbers below
Card numberResult
1Successful payment
2Declined card
3Gateway error
anything elseGeneric failure

Name on card / expiry / CVV: anything in the right format. None of it's validated.

step 4 · verify the chain

✅ Order shows up in Shopify admin → Orders, marked "Paid"
✅ Confirmation email lands at the customer email you typed
✅ Inventory drops by the quantity ordered (if inventory is tracked)
✅ IVU 11.5% line on the receipt
✅ Shipping cost calculated (if you set up rates)

step 5 · cleanup

Refund / cancel test orders so they don't pollute order history (Orders → click test order → Refund)
Re-enable password page if you want storefront private until launch (Online Store → Preferences → Password protection → toggle ON)
DO NOT disable Bogus Gateway yet — auto-disables when client's Shopify Payments goes live after KYC. Leaving it on can't process real money (by definition)
